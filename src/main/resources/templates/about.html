About Paws & Relax

Where passion for pets meets professional grooming excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Paws & Relax started with a simple mission: to create a grooming experience that pets actually enjoy. We realized that many dogs view a trip to the groomer with anxiety. We set out to change that by designing a fear-free, cage-less salon where every pet is treated like family.

Today, we are proud to be the premier grooming destination, offering top-tier spa treatments, breed-specific styling, and most importantly, endless patience and love for your furry best friends.
